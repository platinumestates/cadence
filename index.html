Settings
Tier thresholds, voice, baseline, and the disclaimer.
HR Source
Web Bluetooth pairs directly with the sensor — lowest latency, but only one app can hold the sensor at a time. Pulsoid stream reads the live feed from your Pulsoid session, so Pulsoid and Cadence can run side-by-side.
Generate a token at pulsoid.net/ui/keys (BRO plan only). Scope required: data:heart_rate:read. Stored only in this browser's localStorage.
Voice Cues
Custom audio plays your recorded m4a files for A2, A3, A4, and recovery (A1 and chronic tiers fall back to TTS since no audio is recorded for them). Web SpeechSynthesis uses the browser's built-in voice.
Short ascending chime (C5 → C6) when you cross into a higher tier band — fires before the sustain cue so you know something changed. 30-second debounce per tier.
Cue plays A2 (Breathe / limit orders) for custom audio, or "Is this trade in your plan?" for TTS. Ping plays the A2 chime.
Audio Slots
Upload your own recorded m4a/mp3/wav files per alert. Uploads are stored in this browser only (not cross-device). Slots without custom or bundled audio speak via the browser's built-in voice (TTS). Max 500 KB per file.
Scheduled Voice Alerts
Fires only when the HR sensor is connected — so they don't ring during off hours. Each alert fires once per day max. Customize individual alert audio in the Audio Slots section above.

Cross-Device Sync
The app and this HTML load from cadence-umber.vercel.app on any Chrome/Edge browser. Your settings, Pulsoid token, and custom audio uploads live in this browser's localStorage. Use the buttons below to export a single backup file (includes your uploaded audio encoded as base64) and import it on another device.
Full automatic cloud sync (upload once, plays everywhere without a manual backup step) requires a Firebase project or a Vercel serverless function. Tell me which you want and I'll wire it up.
Baseline
When HR stays below this for 30s after an elevated excursion, a quiet "Nice recovery." cue plays once. Set slightly above your typical relaxed trading HR.
Time spent at or above this BPM accumulates toward chronic-stress tiers. Default 95 bpm.
Cooldowns & Behavior

Disclaimer
Cadence thresholds are behavioral and performance-based, not medical. Sustained resting HR > 155 bpm or consistently downward HRV trends warrant cardiology review — not Cadence configuration adjustment. This app is a trading self-regulation tool, not a diagnostic device.
Before you connect
A few things the Verity Sense is picky about.
!
Force-close the Polar Flow app on your phone.It hogs the Bluetooth connection exclusively. Cadence will fail to pair while Polar Flow is running.
i
Put the Verity Sense into sensor/broadcast mode.Brief button press cycles modes. You want a solid blue LED (green = recording only, will not broadcast). Long press = off.
i
Pin this tab.Right-click the tab → Pin. Chrome's tab discard manager will kill the BT connection when the tab goes idle in the background.
i
The device sleeps after ~1 minute idle.If you can't see it in the pairing dialog, re-press the button and wait for the solid blue LED again.
